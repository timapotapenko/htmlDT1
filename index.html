Dt Crypto: A Social Experiment in Cryptocurrency

The Dt Crypto project is a university experiment designed to explore what happens when all tokens are distributed exclusively through mining. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies, where a portion of tokens is pre-allocated to developers, investors, or marketing, Dt Crypto eliminates any pre-distribution, ensuring completely fair conditions for all participants.

This approach allows us to observe the natural formation of a crypto economy—how users mine, trade, and utilize the token when given equal opportunities from the start. The full transparency of the TON blockchain guarantees open transaction records and real-time tracking of token distribution, making this experiment particularly valuable for analysis.

The project aims to examine the social and economic impact of purely decentralized distribution. This is an important step in understanding how cryptocurrencies can develop without centralized control and what self-regulating mechanisms may emerge within such a system.

Vladyslava Shevchenko, Dmytro Shcherbakov, Taras Kucherov, and Vladyslav Diakiv worked and implemented this project and website specifically for the course 2SE307 Bitcoin and blockchain - praktický seminář - anglicky (2024/2025 ZS) at VŠE in Prague.
